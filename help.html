LIONS PORTABLE OXYGEN MACHINE BANK with Vasai Virar CA’s Parivaar in Vasai Virar Region Portable Oxygen machines for home treatment.

With the continued spread of Covid-19 in the state, waiting lines for hospital admissions are long (2-3 days for regular patients in good hospitals) and bed availability is low. During this waiting period, it would be very helpful if critical patients could get oxygen machines at home.

Keeping this in mind, Lions Club of Navghar Vasai Road with Vasai Virar CA’s Parivaar have come up with a novel project of providing portable oxygen machines for home treatment. In Lions Oxygen Bank, 5 litre capacity portable oxygen machines will be available.

The procedure for availing the services of portable oxygen machine for home treatment is as under:

  • Prescription of a doctor & copy of Aadhar Card of Patient is necessary.
  • Referred by a lions and CA member or a known person of the society.
  • Token charge will be Rs. 100 per day, maximum for 5 days, and can be extended if requested by the doctor.
  • The patient is required to pay for the mask, which is a consumable item.
  • Patient’s relatives are requested for picking up and returning the machine and are having the knowledge of using the oxygen machine.
  • Deposit of Rs.5,000/- is to be paid which is refundable at the time of machine being returned in the condition.
Note: An oximeter to measure the oxygen saturation rate will not be provided along with the oxygen machine requested to buy it.
