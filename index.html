We are Vasai-Virar

This is an unofficial dashboard for the COVID-19 cases in Vasai-Virar area.

We are a team of individuals from Vasai-Virar who thought that we need a dashboard for tracking the spread of Novel Coronavirus in our area.

Important Notes

1. This is a crowd sourced tracker and will be updated once we have a confirmation on the case from an official datasource. We are NOT an official source.

2. Official datasource here means the bulletins from VVCMC and the Collectors Office and Official Social Media handles

3. The number of tested cases is only shared by the authorities and will be updated with the latest figure once we have it from them. We update these numbers as soon as we have them.
